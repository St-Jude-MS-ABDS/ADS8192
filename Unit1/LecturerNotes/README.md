# Unit 1 Lecturer Notes

These notes are instructor-facing companions to the student lecture vignettes.

Use them for:

- pacing and emphasis during live delivery
- reminders about points worth saying informally even if they are not developed in full
- decisions about what to skip or compress when time is tight
- common misconceptions to correct early
